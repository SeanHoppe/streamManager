## Proposal — "Convergence cycle" to move the needle, not the margin

> **Premise.** Today's findings cluster around two structural problems

> (1) the v10 chain is held by a single 5-cycle-deferred seed, and
> (2) the cap-tighten lever wired at v2.7 P1 was falsified at P2 because
> Sonnet's true response distribution is unknown. Standard responses
> are margin-moves: another cap-tighten band (35 / 40 / 45 s), another
> deferral, another consolidation. Below is a single high-leverage
> proposal that collapses the next 2–3 cycles into one.

### The honest read

Every open issue except Seed v2.6-C Path-D P5 is a **side-quest**. The
held-chain depth is 4 issues deep behind a single ~600 LOC seed that
has been deferred 5 consecutive cycles because each cycle picks the
