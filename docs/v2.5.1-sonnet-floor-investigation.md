## Root-cause verdict

**Verdict: A — Measurement artefact.**

**Quoted evidence:**

1. **n=6 re-measure pass_rate = 0.9375 ≥ 0.80** FR-OG-7 floor (full
   sample, no exclusions). Source: `reports/alignment-eval-20260520T092222Z.json`
   `summary.sonnet_pass_rate`.
2. **3 of the 4 n=3 stable-failure rows became unstable at n=6**
   (`frog7-phase-timings-keys-05`, `frog7-cli-worker-send-06`,
   `ambig-block-reset-tag-16`). These were the primary drivers of the
   n=3 floor breach. Their n=3 "stability" was a 3-run unanimous-
   agreement artefact that 6 runs falsifies. Source: n=3 vs n=6
   row table above.
3. **The sole n=6 stable-divergent row (`frog7-wirecli-module-10`)
   is 100% timeout-attributable** (6/6 Sonnet runs hit
   `cli governance timeout (>25.0s); degrading`). Its "stable NONE"
   is the timeout-fallback verdict, not a Sonnet content judgement.
   Source: `reports/alignment-eval-n6-stdout.log` timeout tally.
4. **Excluding timeout-attributable rows lifts pass_rate to 1.000**
   on the n=6 sample (variant (d) above).

No follow-up content fix is required for v2.5.0 tag. v2.5.0 may ship
under verdict-A handling. Two follow-up tracks open:

- **v2.5.0 ship-gate refire** (phase-2, this corrective cycle).
- **Seed v2.5-A: `frog7-wirecli-module-10` content visibility**
  (carry-forward, NOT a v2.5.0 blocker). The row remains
  100%-timeout-degraded so it gives zero information about Sonnet's
  actual judgement on this prompt; treated as a measurement-blind
  spot to be resolved by Seed v2.4-G timeout-instrumentation work
  in v2.6 (per ADR-18 freeze deferral).
