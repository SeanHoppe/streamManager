The sweep modal is a selectable ledger, not an "are you sure?" dialog: every stale row is pre-checked; Protected (open-HITL) and Self rows are shown but structurally un-checkable. Drag the older than slider to widen/narrow the set; press the single saturated Archive N. Each archived row keeps a Restore, and a sweep-level Undo last sweep reverses the whole pass in one click.
