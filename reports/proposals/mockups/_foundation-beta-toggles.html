Promotion path. A BETA feature that proves its worth can later be flipped to default-ON, then have its flag removed entirely (always-on) -- a deliberate per-feature decision in a future cycle. Until then it stays here, opt-in, reversible.

Backed by GET /api/beta/flags + POST /api/beta/flags/{key} (additive, read/write a new beta_flags table) and the betaFlags store. Unknown or unset keys default OFF; a backend error degrades to all-OFF (never silently flips a flag on). Paired label+color on every state (the ON/OFF text is the signal, color reinforces -- never color alone).
