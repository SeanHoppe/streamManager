Why this earns its place

  • Pure GLANCE. The one-line headline (CORPUS: 14/15 ALLOW -- mean 0.97 -- L1) answers "is this session boring enough to ignore?" at the exact instant the operator reaches for the SYNC/ASYNC switch. Zero clicks for the headline.
  • Never a verdict. 1px DASHED border + the literal "ADVISORY -- your decision stands" tag mean it reads as provisional by structure, not chrome. It emits no selection event and sits OFF the verdict hot path (M18).
  • Paired label + color (ADR-18 M4). The dominant verb word + share fraction carry all meaning; the calm-green / amber edge only reinforces. A colorblind operator reads the number and the word. Strip the tint -- the signal survives.
  • Cold shape degrades honestly. n=0 renders "NO HISTORY -- novel shape, earns attention" with no tint claim, rather than a misleading calm verdict. The inverse signal is what makes the calm signal trustworthy.
  • Polarity made visible. The excluded_self readout surfaces SM-self decisions dropped from the corpus (project_slug NOT IN {streamManager}); it shows 0 here, non-zero when relevant.
  • Server-down safety. On fetch error the chip falls back to the offline fixture AND paints a literal "SAMPLE DATA" tag (ConfidenceChip idiom) so the operator is never misled that sample data is live.
  • Optional expand. Enter/Space on the chip's expand button reveals the per-action histogram + n + match kind (exact shape vs nearest k-NN). Esc collapses. Honors prefers-reduced-motion (snap, not slide).
