How to read this mockup

  • Toggle health-sparklines OFF (top right) -- every sparkline strip and the BETA footer chip vanish; the lanes remain (they pre-exist the feature) and ZERO new pollers/SSE handlers are registered.
  • Confidence = the solid load-bearing trace (top 70% of the 18px band). Throughput = the dotted, low-opacity floor wash beneath. The two traces are distinguished by SHAPE, not color alone.
  • Color is the SECOND channel only (M4): slate = healthy, amber = drifting, red = floor-breach -- always paired with the lane badge + the inline text read + the SVG aria-label.
  • Hover OR keyboard-focus a sparkline (Tab to it, then Enter/Space) to open the drawer with the last ~100 decisions; Esc closes and returns focus. The drawer never auto-pops (M2).
  • Lane 2 (harbor-pipeline) is the case the feature exists for: still OBSERVING, no amber badge -- but its confidence trace is visibly sagging, caught before the escalation cluster lands.
