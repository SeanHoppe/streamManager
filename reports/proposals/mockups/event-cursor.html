What the gate does

ON (shown): on boot, if sessionStorage holds a cursor for (session_id, origin), the client calls GET /api/sessions/{id}/events?since=<cursor>&full=1, accumulates digest_delta into the same stores the SSE feeds, then opens /events from the persisted Last-Event-ID. The feed/footer/rail re-materialize at the prior watermark; the badge reads RESUMED.

OFF (default): the refresh discards the in-memory cursor. The feed re-seeds the last 25 decisions and the tallies rebuild from scratch -- the badge reads LIVE and the console briefly looks emptier than reality. Toggle the switch above to preview both.
