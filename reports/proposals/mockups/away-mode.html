What this is

The operator is about to leave the keyboard for 15--60 min. Flip the AWAY pill in the masthead and the monitor goes quiet: events buffer silently in the already-self-excluded stores, HITL rows stay OBSERVING (nothing auto-resolved), no pulsing. A real M2 escalation still breaks through instantly and auto-opens the summary.

On return, ONE Activity Summary overlay replays the gap instead of a scrolled-off feed: escalation timeline, new-agent roster, queued-HITL count, and the away window in minutes. One click on Catch Up clears the buffer + resumes live; Review Pending jumps to the HITL dock; Esc dismisses.

Try it

1. Click the PRESENT pill -- it flips to AWAY, the frames calm, and the buffered counter climbs.

2. Click the AWAY pill again to return -- because a real escalation buffered, the Activity Summary auto-shows.

3. In the overlay: Catch Up clears the buffer + resumes live; Review Pending focuses the HITL dock; Esc dismisses; Tab cycles the 3 footer buttons (focus is trapped, then restored to the pill).
