What you are looking at

Over a multi-week soak the rail accrues dead non-SM lanes -- ended sessions, crashed PIDs, one-off probe runs -- that crowd the calm rail and dilute the ACTIVE tally. The only cleanup today is a hand-written SQL DELETE against gov.db: irreversible and hostile to a glance-first operator.

This beta reframes cleanup as archive a drawer you can reopen, not irreversible surgery:

  1. Flip stale-cleanup ON (default OFF, toggled in Settings > BETA features).
  2. A hairline Archive stale button appears in the rail footer -- only while stale lanes exist.
  3. Click it -> a right-anchored PREVIEW modal shows exactly what will be archived, before any commit.
  4. Drag the hours slider; the count re-previews live. Then click the one saturated CONFIRM ARCHIVE.
  5. Lanes collapse out of the rail; each archived row keeps a RESTORE button -- an over-cut costs one click.
